Experience the best of Baguio with our freshly brewed coffee.


Arabica Beans

Baguio is known for its high-quality Arabica beans, which thrive in the city's favorable climate and high altitude. Arabica beans have a delicate flavor profile with subtle notes of fruitness and floral undertones.

Robusta Beans

Baguio also produces robusta beans, known for their robust flavor and higher caffeine content compared to Arabica beans. Robusta beans offer bold tastes that can stan up well in various brewing methods.

Specialty Coffee Shops

Baguio is home to numerous specialty coffee shops that offer a wide variety of coffee made from locally sourced beans. These cozy cafes provide a unique coffee experience with their expertly brewed cups and curated coffee blends.
